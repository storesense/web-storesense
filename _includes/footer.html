About us

Storesense develops premium analytics services based on passive Wi-Fi scanning. Our solutions provide business value to retailers, shopping malls, event organisers, event venues, sponsors and exhibitors. Our mission is not to provide data, but actual business advice that helps our clients address and resolve their business challenges. Storesense has tracked over 6 million visitor journeys in the last 6 months alone.
